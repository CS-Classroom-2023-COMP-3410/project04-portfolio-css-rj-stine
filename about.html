Learn All About Me!

Hello! My name is RJ Stine, I am a current senior here at the University of Denver. In my almost four years here, I have been working towards a major in computer science and I also currently have a minor in mathematics as well as a minor in psychology.

I lived the first 21 years of my life in a small town called Placentia located on the California coast but decided I needed a change when it came time to apply for colleges. Since coming out here, my whole family has followed and I now call Estes Park, CO my home whenever I'm not in school. Outside of school, I enjoy reading in my spare time, collecting records, playing video games, photography, and enjoying time with my friends.

Along with my time at DU I am also a brother of the Delta Upsilon Chapter of the Phi Gamma Delta (FIJI) brotherhood here on campus. We participate in and host various community events and strive to contribute positively to the Denver community.

Some skills I've picked up:

  • Use of programming languages
  • Being able to speak Italian
  • Ability to solve a Rubik's Cube
  • A keen ability for making Spotify playlists
  • Very good at puzzles
